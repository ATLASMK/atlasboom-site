US tons

Rated lifting capacity in US tons (1 US ton = 2,000 lb). The most common shorthand on American job sites.

Rated capacity

Peak rated lift in pounds at the minimum boom configuration. Capacity drops as reach extends. See each model's load chart for the full curve.

Max reach

Horizontal working distance at full hydraulic extension. AT-100 and AT-120 figures are published with the load chart on request.

Boom config

Hydraulic plus manual sections. 5+2 means five hydraulic and two manual extensions. Higher counts give longer reach at lower tip capacity.
